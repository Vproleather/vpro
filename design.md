## üé¨ **4. Motion & Interaction DNA**

Interaction design is subtle, cinematic, and emotionally deliberate.

### **Motion Principles**

* **Ease: Expo or quintic** ‚Äî premium, high-end animations
* **Entrance animations**: fade+translate, never bouncy
* **Scroll transitions**: parallax, soft opacity gradients
* **Hover states**: micro-scale, underline sweeps, color inversions

### **Interaction Tone**

* Motion should feel like *breathing*, not bouncing
* Rewards curiosity without overwhelming the user
* Every interaction feels **crafted**, not templated
